The Lightning Phantom II NEW

The Phantom combines the overall geometry and seat of the P-38 with solid chromoly mono-tube construction for a fast bike that’s durable, comfortable and very affordable. Now available also as a midracer.

The Lightning P-38

NEW Avid disc brakes on XT model

The legendary P-38 is still our flagship and our second-lightest recumbent (only our R-84 is lighter). A stiff, strong patented space frame design, plus the patent-pending Ergofit seat make the P-38 one of the finest recumbents you can own for touring, racing, and recreational riding. Choose our standard mesh seat or the midracer configuration. Also available as a frameset. Made in the USA

The Lightning P-38 Voyager

Now you can take it with you! We start with our legendary P-38, and then add frame couplers and a hard-shell carrying case. Assemble or dissassemble in as few as thirty minutes. Made in the USA

The Lightning F-40

For racers and high-performance enthusiasts, the record breaking fully-faired F-40 can’t be beat. In fact it holds some 12 world speed records. Also available as a frameset. Made in the USA

The Lightning R-84
