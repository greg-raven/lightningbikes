Brake levers

Touring or mountain type levers are best choices for optimal hand position. We use Tektro or Sensir levers, modified to fit the curve of the Lightning drop handlebar. If you are buying a frameset, you can obtain these modified levers at the same time.

Other accessories

Other common bicycle accessories can be easily added to any of our bicycles:

  • Water bottle cages
  • Battery-operated lighting systems
  • Most types of rear racks and bags
  • Most cyclometers (some may require extra-long sensor cable)
