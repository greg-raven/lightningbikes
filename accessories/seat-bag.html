Accessories: Slip-on seat bag

Custom made for the Lightning, the Seat Bag quickly and easily slips over the top of the seat to carry everything needed on a day ride.

It also includes slots for two 1.5 liter hydration bladders (available separately), and a small key and wallet pocket. Two slits at the top of the bag for hydration tubes and earphone cords. A carry handle allows you to take the bag with you after you park your Lightning.

Capacity 700 cubic inches
Weight: 0.5 pounds
Colors: Black, blue, red, yellow, bright orange
