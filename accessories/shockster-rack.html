Shockster rack mount adapters

You can mount a rear rack on your Shockster-equipped Lightning, you just need our Shockster rack mount adapter kit.

Warning: If panniers are used with the rear suspension, only use the Lightning brand panniers as these place the weight forward. Other types of panniers will place the weight towards the rear and cause the suspension unit to separate leading to damage or personal injury.
