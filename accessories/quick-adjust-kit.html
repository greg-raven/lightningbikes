Accessories: RAAM-tested quick-adjust kit

This amazing kit makes your Phantom or P-38 easily adjustable to fit various riders in your family! The idea came from RAAM (the non-stop Race Across America), where Tim needed a way to fit the racing bike to multiple riders, and change quickly among them.

The chain take-up device option lets you adjust the crank boom without changing the chain length. Includes quick release skewers for the crank and handlebar adjustments! Perfect for quick adjustments for different users of the same bike.

Weight: 0.6 pounds
