Party!

Mark your calendars for Saturday, March 9th, 2013.

For most people, this is the date of the Solvang Century. For Lightning enthusiasts, it’s the date of our 30th Anniversary party for the Lightning P-38. We’re having a barbecue lunch, and anyone riding a Lightning gets in free. If you’re riding in the Solvang Century, you can stop in for a bite to eat before riding back to the finish in Solvang.

Of course we will have our latest creations available for test rides, including the 30th-Anniversary edition of our legendary P-38.
