Choosing a Dealer

We always recommend that you find a dealer close to you who can give you the personal service you need, if possible. For some buyers, this may mean traveling to visit a dealer in a nearby city or state. We are expanding our dealer network, so check back here often for new dealers as they join the team.

If you don’t live near a dealer, you are welcome to order directly from the factory. Prices are the same as they would be from a dealer (unless the dealer might be offering a special — call them and see!) and you’ll have to be able to do final assembly, adjustment and test the bike yourself. We can help you by phone, but we’ll never be able to do all that a dealer can do.
