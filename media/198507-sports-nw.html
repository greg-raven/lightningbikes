The Lightning X-2 speeds across the finish line at the Seattle to Portland Ride in 7:31:42 — establishing an STP record. Pete Penseyres, winner of the 1984 Race Across America, powered the aerodynamic vehicle for the 192 miles and won $1,000 in challenge money for coming in under the alloted time. […]
