Phantom II

The Phantom combines the overall geometry and seat of the P-38 with solid chromoly mono-tube construction for a fast bike that’s durable, comfortable and very affordable. Now available also as a midracer.

P-38

NEW Hydraulic disc brakes and carbon forks

The legendary P-38 is still our flagship and our second-lightest recumbent (only our R-84 is lighter). A stiff, strong patented space frame design, plus the patent-pending Ergofit seat make the P-38 one of the finest recumbents you can own for touring, racing, and recreational riding. Choose our standard mesh seat or the midracer configuration. Also available as a frameset.
