2006 Furnace Creek 508

In 2006, Tim Woudenberg (shown below) set the current overall recumbent record in the grueling Furnace Creek 508. For this event, Tim rode solo, completing the course in 31 hours, 50 minutes, 35 seconds. Tim’s mount was a Lightning F-90.
