Sliding forward in the seat

A customer sent in this solution:

“I put a piece of foam about 4 inches wide under the foam pad that comes with the P-38 that seems to help a great deal. I did 50+ this past Saturday with no real problems, I had a number of hills on the ride and made them as good or maybe easier than on my wedgie. I’m beginning to like this thing.”
