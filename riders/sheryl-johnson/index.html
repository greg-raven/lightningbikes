Dear Tim and Bob (and anyone else who worked on my P-38 to Voyager conversion),

I am one very happy customer and sing the praises of my Voyager to everyone who asks about it … and a LOT of people ask about this bike! When I bought the used P-38 this summer, I was impressed with everything about it from the quality of the build to the quality and comfort of the ride. Now, my husband and I are in awe of the engineering and thought that went into the Voyager retrofit. When we brought my Voyager home in its suitcase, we had it unpacked, fully reassembled and ride-ready in about an hour — easy! With practice we will probably be faster next time.

Here are a few photos taken just after you did the retrofit: (1) Bob, Tim and me in front of Lightning Cycle Dynamics with the Voyager packed in its suitcase; (2) Reassembled and out for a ride on a beautiful autumn day in the Napa Valley.

Thank you so very much! I look forward to many happy miles, in many interesting places, on my Voyager, and will recommend a Lightning bike to anyone interested in a recumbent.

Sincerely,

Sheryl Johnson
