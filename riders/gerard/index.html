Gérard’s Lightning P-38

Here are photos of my 10-year-old P-38. It was my first recumbent bike and it is still wonderful. I use it now with two panniers to carry groceries and when I need to go in town. It is so versatile and fast that I have no problems in traffic. Before I had the velomobile, I would mount the F-40 fairing and I could increase my speed by 30%, but it was very sensitive in the wind, so now I use the fairing only when racing.

Gérard
