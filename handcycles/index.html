Lightning Handcycles

The legend continues

The Lightning handcycle has numerous unique features for maximum performance, some which are found on our legendary P-38 recumbent bicycle.

  • Light but rigid space frame transfers all power to the drive wheel, no more torque steer.
  • Rider can push against the shoulder high seat back to deliver maximum power to the pedals.
  • Cast aluminum hand grips are molded to perfectly fit your hand for maximum power output with no discomfort.
  • Full mesh seat back is cool, and does not compress like foam for maximum power output.
  • Ergofit seat shape for maximum comfort.
  • Standard dual control shifters allow on the fly shifting and braking, so you can always keep the power on!
  • The unique lean-steering mechanism consists of aircraft rod-ends which allows the rider to steer by leaning. Thus full power can continue to be applied while turning.
  • Lean-steering naturally shifts your weight inward for maximum cornering speeds.
  • The lean-steering axis is adjustable to suit your riding style, from high speed straight aways to tight cornering.
  • The learn steer system includes automatic centering springs and hydraulic stabilizers, which are fully adjustable to match your riding style.
  • Handlebars below the seat can be used for better control in tight turns and fast downhills.
  • A third brake lever is mounted on the handlebars.
  • A fourth brake lever is mounted to the frame, this also doubles as a parking brake.
  • 20 inch back wheel reduce weight and wind resistance.
  • Rear wheels are aligned vertically with no camber, for minimum rolling resistance and maximum tread life.
  • Optional Hayes hydraulic disc brake prevents front rim overheating on long, steep downhills.
  • Two frames sizes for a perfect fit.
  • You choice of custom colors.

All this adds up to a lightweight, comfortable high quality handcycle that is fast in any terrain, from hills to curves to flat straights.
